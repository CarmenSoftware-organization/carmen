# Product Management Module - Business Requirements

This directory contains business requirements documentation for the Product Management module.

## Documents

- **[product-ba.md](./product-ba.md)** - Comprehensive business analysis for product catalog management

## Module Overview

The Product Management module maintains the master product catalog. Key features include:

- Product catalog management
- Product specifications
- Unit of measure (UOM) management
- Product categories and classifications
- Product pricing
- Product availability tracking
- Supplier product relationships
- Product lifecycle management

## Key Stakeholders

- Product Managers
- Procurement Team
- Inventory Managers
- Sales Team
- Finance Team
